Ethical Dilemma in IT

Misuse of Administrative Privileges

What happened: A system administrator accessed confidential user data without authorization.

Who was involved: The administrator, company leadership, and affected users.

Initial response: Access was revoked, an internal audit launched, and security policies reinforced.

Reflection: This case highlights the need for integrity, accountability, and spiritual self-governance in IT roles of trust.
